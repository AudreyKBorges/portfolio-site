About Me

I'm a recent graduate of the Bachelor of Science program in Web Development and Design at Champlain College in Burlington, Vermont, and I am familiar with front-end and back-end technologies. My current stack is pure HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I have also worked with WordPress, Figma, Invision, Python, SQL, MySQL, sass/scss, Bootstrap, and Symfony.

Houston, Texas is currently my home base, and the weather is hot but so is the art scene. When I'm not building an awesome new web project or working at theCoderSchool teaching kids to code, I go to art museums and art shows, enjoy coffee--love my cold brew with oat milk, and get my nature fix at one of Houston's many beautiful parks.
